MISSION

Our ultimate aim is to help small and perhaps struggling businesse survive and even thrive in this once-in-a lifetime pandemic environment. The federal and state governments have given substantial amounts of money and resources to prop up businesses to survive this one-in a lifetime pandemic. Many businesses would have gone under but for this intervention. However, the capital infusion money is not free. Due diligence is being done to ensure that taxpayer money is not squandered by quack businesses or businesses with quack business practices. Case in point is the Florida business owner who purchased a Lamborghini with his SBA PPP loan. However, just like filing of taxes, a business must prove to the IRS that it is entitled to a quantum of refund or a minimization of its tax liability. FANALYTIQA aspires to help small businesses legitimately maximize their forgiven amounts. The ridiculously low fee of 0.5% (zero-point five percent) is meant to put your mind at ease, as well as leave your pocketbook unaffected. This oversight fee for a ‘second opinion’ is a smart step in ensuring that your business gets the maximum possible forgiveness amount.

OBJECTIVE

Currently, our objective is to help the owners of small businesses keep the MAXIMUM amount possible from the Small Business administration (SBA) Payment Protection Program (PPP) Loan.

ANALYTICS

Be confident that the complex PPP forgiveness calculations have been done correctly, with the right methods. Customized to your needs, our consulting services help support your application and maximize your forgiveness amount.
